From fresh spring pastels to rich autumn hues, each season inspires distinct makeup aesthetics. Understanding seasonal color theory, texture preferences, and trending finishes helps you stay current while adapting looks to weather conditions and natural light changes throughout the year.

Transitioning Between Seasons

Master the art of adapting your makeup as seasons change

1
Update Your Base

Switch foundation formulas and shades with the seasons. Lighter coverage in warm months, more coverage in cold. Adjust undertones as your skin tone changes.

2
Rotate Your Palette

Keep a seasonal color rotation: pastels for spring, brights for summer, warm tones for autumn, and jewels for winter to stay current.

3
Adjust Textures

Lighter, dewy finishes for spring and summer; richer, more dimensional finishes for autumn and winter create seasonally appropriate looks.

Transition Tip

Build a capsule makeup collection with versatile neutrals and add seasonal statement pieces. This keeps your routine fresh without complete overhauls.
